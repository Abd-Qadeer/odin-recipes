Chili Con Carne

Description

In this traditional recipe, ground beef is browned to perfection, releasing its savory aromas and adding a depth of flavor to the dish. The beef is then combined with a medley of ingredients such as onions, garlic, tomatoes, and a blend of spices like chili powder, cumin, and paprika. These spices infuse the chili with a smoky and slightly spicy taste that is balanced by the sweetness of the tomatoes. To add a touch of heat, some recipes include jalapenos or other chili peppers.

The addition of beans, typically kidney beans, adds both texture and nutritional value to the chili. They provide a creamy and slightly earthy element that complements the meat and spices. The chili is simmered slowly, allowing the flavors to meld together and develop into a rich and satisfying dish. Whether served on its own, topped with cheese and sour cream, or accompanied by cornbread, chili con carne is a beloved comfort food that is sure to warm both body and soul.

Ingredients

  • 1kg 5% fat minced beef
  • 1 and a half brown onions
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 2 tins of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin of kidney beans
  • 1 tin of baked beans
  • Tomato puree
  • White rice
  • Garlic granules
  • Chili powder
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Oregano
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • 2 chilies

Steps

  1. Heat a large frying pan and add some olive oil to the pan
  2. Finely chop 1 and a half onions and add to the pan for 5-10 minutes
  3. Chop the bell peppers while the onions are cooking
  4. After the 5-10 mins of cooking the onions, you can add the beef to the pan and break it up, cook the beef till it browns
  5. Add garlic granules midway while cooking the beef
  6. Once the beef is cooked transfer it to a big saucepan and cook on a low heat
  7. Add your 2 tins of chopped tomatoes to the saucepan
  8. Add the baked beans to the saucepan
  9. Drain and add the kidney beans to the saucepan
  10. Put the frying pan back on the heat and cook the bell peppers for a little while it has some colour then transfer to the saucepan
  11. Add a generous amount of tomato puree to the saucepan
  12. Add the spice mix to the saucepan: 4 tablespoons of chili powder, add 1 tablespoon of oregano, add 2 tablespoons of cumin,1 teaspoon of smoked paprika,1 teaspoon of salt, 3 teaspoons of garlic granules, half a teaspoon of black pepper
  13. Put the saucepan's lid on low heat and put the heat on low, and allow it to cook for 45 mins-2hours, stirring occasionally
  14. Start cooking the white rice 10-15 mins before the chili con carne is done
  15. The final step is to serve the chili con carne with the rice on a plate.
