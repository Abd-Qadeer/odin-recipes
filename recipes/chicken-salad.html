Chicken and rice

Description

Introducing our high-protein chicken salad recipe! Packed with nutritious ingredients, this salad is not only delicious but also a great choice for those looking to increase their protein intake. With a combination of lean chicken breast and Greek yogurt, this recipe provides a substantial amount of protein to fuel your body and support muscle growth and repair.

In addition to the protein-rich chicken, our salad features a medley of fresh vegetables like celery, red onion, bell pepper, and cucumber, adding a burst of flavor and crunch. To enhance the taste and creaminess, we use a mixture of Greek yogurt and a touch of mayonnaise, creating a creamy dressing that complements the ingredients perfectly. Topped with a sprinkle of fresh parsley and a squeeze of lemon juice, this salad is a refreshing and satisfying option for a healthy lunch or dinner. Whether you enjoy it on its own or as a filling for sandwiches or wraps, our high-protein chicken salad is a versatile and nutritious choice that will leave you feeling satisfied and energized.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast,46g protein, 276 calories
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt, 23g protein, 130 calories
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise, 0g protein, 360 calories
  • 1/4 cup diced celery, 0g protein, 4 calories
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion 0g protein, 16 calories
  • 1/4 cup diced bell pepper, 0g protein, 9 calories
  • 1/4 cup diced cucumber, 0g protein, 4 calories
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, 0g protein, 11 calories
  • 1 cup of broccoli
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice 0g protein, 4 calories
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Total Macros: Protein: 69g, Carbohydrates: 0g ,Fat: 22g. Total Calories: 814 calories.

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the Greek yogurt and mayonnaise. Mix well until smooth.
  2. Add the diced chicken breast to the bowl and mix until the chicken is coated with the yogurt-mayonnaise mixture.
  3. Add the celery, red onion, bell pepper, cucumber, parsley and cooked broccoli to the bowl. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.
  4. Drizzle the lemon juice over the salad and season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix again to evenly distribute the flavors.
  5. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Serve the chicken salad chilled and enjoy!
